How To Play

Match 10+ of one animal to clear that herd, earn coins, and let gravity start chains.

Gold-frame mission tetrads can show up in Next. Hit the mission goal, then clear the pulsing reward-coin herd within 10 settles or the run ends.

Touchscreen Controls

← or → tap the left or right side to nudge one column.

↔ drag sideways to steer while the tetrad falls.

↓ drag down to speed the drop.

↺ swipe up on the left half to rotate counterclockwise. ↻ swipe up on the right half to rotate clockwise.

⇄ double tap or press and hold to swap the falling tetrad with Next. You get one swap per settle.

Laptop Controls

←/→ or A/D = move. ↓ or S = soft drop. Space or F = slam.

↑, W, X, or E = ↻. Q or Z = ↺.

C = swap with Next. P = pause. R = restart.

Scoring And Multipliers

10+ herds earn size bonuses: +2, +3, +5, +8, +13, and so on. Once that herd bonus reaches +55, it slows into +10, +15, +20 steps.

Chain clears add a separate combo payout that starts at +5 for x2 and keeps climbing from there.

🥚 doubles that herd once per egg. 2 eggs = x4. 3 eggs = x8.

💩 halves that herd once per turd. 2 turds = ÷4. Eggs and turds stack together.
