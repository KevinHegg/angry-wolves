How To Play

Match 10+ of one animal to clear that herd. After a herd clears, border squares turn into the strongest touching herd, then animals fall, and chains repeat.

Mission specials use the real Next queue. If a special is in Next, you can swap it, hold it, and plan around it. Hit the goal, then clear the pulsing reward-coin herd within 10 settles or the run ends.

Touchscreen Controls

Tap the left or right side to nudge one column.

Drag sideways to steer. Drag down to speed the drop.

Swipe up on the left half for ↺. Swipe up on the right half for ↻.

Tap the Next card to swap. Press and hold is still there as a backup. You get one swap per settle.

Laptop Controls

←/→ or A/D = move. ↓ or S = soft drop. Space or F = slam.

↑, W, X, or E = ↻. Q or Z = ↺.

C = swap with Next. P = pause. R = restart.

Scoring And Multipliers

10+ herds earn size bonuses: +2, +3, +5, +8, +13, and so on. Once that herd bonus reaches +55, it slows into +10, +15, +20 steps.

Chain clears add a separate combo payout that starts at +5 for x2 and keeps climbing from there.

🥚 doubles that herd once per egg. 2 eggs = x4. 3 eggs = x8.

💩 halves that herd once per turd. 2 turds = ÷4. Eggs and turds stack together.

Legacy Wildcards

These only return if the legacy global-special settings are turned back on.

Mission Specials

The mission brief shows this run's two gold-frame specials before you start. They still arrive through the real Next queue, so if one is in Next you can swap it, hold it, or duck it.
